Why TeleChurn?

In the telecommunications industry it's crucial to minimize customer churn for businesses to thrive. The rate at which customers switch providers, also known as churn has an impact, on both revenue and brand reputation. By understanding the reasons, behind customer churn companies can make informed decisions to decrease attrition and improve satisfaction levels.
